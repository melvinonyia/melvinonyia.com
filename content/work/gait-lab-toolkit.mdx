## What I built

A small command-line toolkit that wraps the gait-lab marker-set conversion,
event detection, and step-segmentation we kept rewriting from scratch in every
study. Ships with a typed schema for trial metadata so downstream notebooks can
trust the shape of what they load.

The tool was built for our own use, but the shape of the API turned out to be
general enough that two collaborators picked it up for their own labs.
